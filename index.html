To purchase $POW using Phantom, first set up your Phantom wallet. Download the Phantom extension from the official website and install it in your browser. Once your wallet is set up, fund it by transferring Solana ($SOL) from an exchange or another wallet to your Phantom Solana address.

Connect to DEX

Next, connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Jupiter,  or Raydium. Open the DEX +website and click on "Connect Wallet," selecting Phantom from the options.
